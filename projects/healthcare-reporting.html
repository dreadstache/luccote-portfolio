Analytics Engineering

Healthcare Reporting & Workflow Automation

A portfolio-safe overview of SQL reporting, dashboarding, and Microsoft 365 automation work supporting healthcare operations.

The problem

Operational and clinical reporting depended on complex data relationships, recurring manual steps, and reliable delivery across several systems.

What I built

SQL-based reporting workflows, Crystal Reports, Power BI outputs, and Microsoft 365 automations connecting forms, SharePoint, and SQL-backed processes.

My contribution

I designed queries, reshaped one-to-many clinical data, developed parameterized reports, diagnosed workflow failures, and translated operational needs into maintainable technical solutions.

Tools and technologies

SQL Server, T-SQL, SSMS, Crystal Reports, Power BI, SharePoint, Power Automate, Microsoft Forms.

Outcome

The work improved data accessibility, reduced avoidable manual handling, and supported clearer operational decision-making. No patient or confidential organizational data is shown here.
